About Us

An inviting pub & restaurant serving seasonal menus, tempting cocktails, well-kept real ales & fine wines located in the picturesque village of Chappel. Surrounded by beautiful countryside& under the constant gaze of the striking viaductthe Swan Inn is a Grade II listed building with courtyard, gardens, al fresco terrace & heated marquee which are perfect for all day dining & unwinding.

Whether you just fancy breakfast or a pub lunch, have a meeting with business colleagues for coffee, are planning a special night or simply having drinks with friends, the White Hart is the perfect choice.

Modern pub food & fantastic Chef's Specials are made from scratch using the finest, freshest ingredients hand sourced from Billingsgate, Smithfield & Spitalfields Market in addition to using local suppliers. In addition to our in-house dining options, we also offer Take Away & Delivery including our popular Sunday Roasts.

Drinks to complement our food include cocktails complete with seasonal specials, draught beers & lagers, Real Ale, wines from around the world including eight by the glass, a selection of soft drinks, coffees & teas.

Swan Inn also holds weekly 2 For 1 deals, offers the best Sunday Roasts, Food Theme Events, Children’s Menu, Beer Festivals & more making it the ideal family local.
